I am a Master's student in the particle theory group at the Yukawa Institute for Theoretical Physics (YITP), Kyoto University. I joined YITP in 2026 after studying applied physics engineering at Hokkaido University (2022–2026).

My research interests are in theoretical high-energy physics (hep-th), particularly **holography and the AdS/CFT correspondence** — the conjecture that a theory of quantum gravity in a higher-dimensional "bulk" spacetime is exactly equivalent to a quantum field theory living on its lower-dimensional boundary. I am currently building up the tools to work on this correspondence and its extensions.

*In plain terms:* I study mathematical "dictionaries" that translate between theories of gravity and theories of particles, in the hope of better understanding how gravity and quantum mechanics fit together.

Feel free to reach out by email if you'd like to discuss anything related to my research.
